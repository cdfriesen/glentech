About Us

We are a full service computer sales & repair facility. The business was started in 1995 from Chester & Wanda Friesen's home. In April 2006 we opened in Willows at 1025 W Wood St. In November of 2008 we moved to 1030 W Wood St, Suite 3. In October of 2014 we moved to our present location at 251 N Villa Ave.

Our aim is to give our customers as pleasant an experience as we can in providing solutions to their computer problems. Traditionally computer techs tend to be somewhat "stand-offish" towards the general public who are not computer savvy. We realize that computers have become an almost standard appliance in many households. Computers often seem very mysterious in their operation. We do our best to bridge that gap and help customers through their computer problems.

We also provide a line of business forms and checks along with many other items. Stop by and see us, let our experience work for you.
